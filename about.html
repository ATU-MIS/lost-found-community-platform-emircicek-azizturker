About Lost & Found Community

Lost & Found Community is a community-driven platform designed to help people reconnect with their lost belongings and return found items to their rightful owners.

The platform brings together students, staff, and community members in a single space where lost and found items can be reported, searched, and claimed easily. By encouraging cooperation and responsibility, the system helps reduce permanent losses and strengthens trust within the community.

Users can post lost item reports with detailed location and time information, browse found items, and securely contact each other through the system. The goal is to make the recovery process fast, transparent, and accessible for everyone.

Lost & Found Community promotes social responsibility, collaboration, and digital accessibility by providing a simple and modern interface that works across devices.
